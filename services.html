Our Services

Every Family Member Is Welcome

Comprehensive Dental Care

The quest for a clean, healthy mouth can be a difficult one, especially if you are unsure about how to begin. Technology continues to advance alongside the many options you have. Let the staff at Wagner Family Dentistry narrow the field and start you on the right path.

Our dentist and his team of professionals will check your teeth, work with you to keep you informed about your mouth's current state, and create a plan to keep your mouth clean and healthy.

We offer the following services to you and your family

General Dentistry

Complete dental services for adults and children.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Including professional whitening treatments.

Cleanings

Routine cleanings to maintain optimal oral health.

Complete & Partial Dentures

Custom-fitted denture solutions for a natural look.

Complete Dental Exams

Thorough evaluations and oral cancer screening.

Crowns & Bridges

Restore function and replace missing teeth.

Dental X-Rays

Digital imaging for accurate diagnosis.

Extractions

Safe and comfortable tooth removal when needed.

Implants

Permanent solutions for missing teeth.

Preventive Care

Protect your smile with proactive treatments.

Root Canals

Save damaged teeth and relieve pain.
